Breakfast special: Saussage and eggs

1. Take a pan and a lid
2. Add oil
3. Heat pan
4. Add saussage
5. Wait until the saussage shows brown color
6. Reduce heat and turn saussige to other side
7. Add 2 eggs
8. Cover pan by lid and don't touch eggs until they are ready
9. Enjoy the saussage with 2 airy soft eggs
